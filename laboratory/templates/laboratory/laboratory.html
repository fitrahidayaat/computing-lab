Computing Laboratory

Since 2014 under Intelligence, Computing, Multimedia (ICM) expertise group, School of Computing, Telkom University this laboratory held any academic events to improve and develop skills of School of Computing, Telkom University

Computing Laboratory
Vision

To be a laboratory that improve knowledge in algorithm and programming for either members or non members. And also able to empower resources in the laboratory to support research programs conducted by School of Computing, Telkom University lecturers.

Mission
  1. Organizing learning routinely for laboratory's members also provides assignments as a form of practice from the material that has been taught.
  2. Preparing all available resources in the laboratory to help the lecturer research, especially lecturers at ICM expertise group, School of Computing, Telkom University.
  3. Maintaining relationships between laboratory members and maintaining comfort & conducive atmosphere in the laboratory that can increases the productivity of members and visitors of the laboratory.
History

The ICM expertise group at that time there were only Artificial Intelligence laboratory and Multimedia laboratory, so to complement the needs of the ICM expertise group, Computing laboratory were established, expected to handle fields that could not be handled by laboratories that already exists, such as cryptography, formal methods, bio-inspired computing, and others.
